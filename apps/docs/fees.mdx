Additional fees apply to digital products hosted and sold on Whop to cover the costs of running the platform, customer support, and other administrative services.

| Type        | Fee | Notes                                                                    |
| :---------- | :-- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hosting** | 3%  | Paid by creators (businesses hosting their digital product on Whop)      |
| **Service** | 5%  | Paid by buyers (end customers buying access to digital products on Whop) |

## Foreign Exchange

For international transactions processed in currencies other than USD, an additional foreign exchange fee applies.

| Service | Fee | Notes                                                    |
| :------ | :-- | :------------------------------------------------------- |
| **FX**  | 1%  | Applied when customer payments settle from non-USD to USD |

## Payouts

An overview of the available payout methods on Whop, including processing times and fees.

### Standard payouts

| Method                                       | Fee               | Processing Time    |
| :------------------------------------------- | :---------------- | :----------------- |
| **Standard ACH (US Only)**                   | \$2.50            | 3 business days    |
| **Next-Day ACH (US Only)**                   | 2% \+ \$1.00      | 1 business day     |
| **Bank Wire (US Only)**                      | \$23.00           | Same business day  |
| **International Bank Deposit & Cash Pickup** | 1%                | Varies by location |

### Instant payouts

| Method             | Fee               | Processing Time        |
| :----------------- | :---------------- | :--------------------- |
| **RTP (Instant)**  | 4% \+ \$1.00      | Real-time              |
| **Venmo**          | 5%                | Minutes                |
| **Cryptocurrency** | 5%                | 10-60 minutes          |

<Tip>
  For international bank deposits or cash pick up, Whop charges an additional 1%.
</Tip>

## Additionals

Whop charges fees to cover fraud detection, payment optimization, and subscription billing.

| Service                     | Fee     | Notes                                         |
| :-------------------------- | :------ | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Billing & Subscriptions** | 0.4%    | Applied to recurring subscription payments    |
| **Payment Orchestration**   | \$0.03  | Transaction routing and optimization          |
| **3D Secure**               | \$0.03  | Enhanced authentication for card payments     |
| **Fraud Detection (Radar)** | \$0.07  | Advanced fraud prevention and risk analysis   |
| **Network Tokens**          | \$0.03  | Secure tokenization for stored payment methods |

<Tip>
  Learn more about how sales tax and VAT are handled on Whop [here](/apps/docs/payments/taxes).
</Tip>

## Dispute & risk management

Fees apply only when disputes occur or when you opt into premium protections (Early Dispute Alerts).

| Service                 | Fee  | When Applied                                                        |
| :---------------------- | :--- | :------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Dispute Fee**         | \$15 | Charged when a chargeback is filed against your account             |
| **Early Dispute Alert** | \$29 | Optional service to resolve disputes before they become chargebacks |

## Other payment methods

If you need to pay affiliates at scale or pay your teammates, Whop's suite of platform tools make it easy to distribute earnings and incentives.

| Transfer Type              | Fee | Notes                                         |
| :------------------------- | :-- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Affiliate payments**     | 3%  | Payments to affiliate partners                |
| **Bounty payment**         | 3%  | Task-based reward payments                    |
| **Team member payment**    | 1%  | Payments to team members                      |
| **Payments between users** | 3%  | Payment to another user for a good or service |

## Merchant of Record

When you sell through Whop, we act as the Merchant of Record. That means we handle all payment processing, chargebacks, sales tax, and compliance on your behalf.

<Note>
  - All fees are subject to change with 30 days notice
  - Foreign exchange (FX) rates are applied at current market rates for international transactions
  - Tax handling may vary by jurisdiction and transaction type
  - Some fees may be capped at certain amounts - see individual service terms for details
</Note>
