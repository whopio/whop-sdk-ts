## Transactions

Whop processes payments from your customers, applies platform and processing fees, and deposits the rest into your account. Below is a breakdown of payment method fees, platform fees, and how they apply to you and your buyers.

| Payment Method               | Fee             | Notes                                                       |
| :--------------------------- | :-------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Card Processing (Global)** | 2.9% \+ \$0.30  | Applied to all credit and debit card transactions worldwide |
| **ACH Processing**           | 0.8% (max \$10) | Applies to payments from US bank accounts.                  |
| **Buy Now, Pay Later**       | 15%             | Covers Affirm, Afterpay, Klarna, Sezzle, SplitIt, and Zip   |

<Tip>
  2.5% \+ \$0.30 card processing fees for qualified businesses. Contact us to learn more.
</Tip>

Whop charges additional fees specifically for businesses hosting digital products on the platform.

| Service     | Fee | Notes                                                                    |
| :---------- | :-- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hosting** | 3%  | Paid by creators (businesses hosting their digital product on Whop)      |
| **Service** | 5%  | Paid by buyers (end customers buying access to digital products on Whop) |

## Payouts

An overview of the available payout methods on Whop, including processing times and fees. 

### Standard payouts

| Method                  | Fee                 | Processing Time   |
| :---------------------- | :------------------ | :---------------- |
| **Next-Day ACH**        | 0.5% (max \$10,000) | 1 business day    |
| **Same-Day ACH**        | 2.5% (max \$10,000) | Same business day |
| **Bank Wire (US Only)** | \$25.00             | Same business day |

### Instant payouts

| Method             | Fee               | Processing Time        |
| :----------------- | :---------------- | :--------------------- |
| **Instant Pay**    | 5% (max \$10,000) | Minutes (push-to-card) |
| **Zelle / RTP**    | 5% (max \$10,000) | Real-time              |
| **Mobile Wallet**  | At cost           | Varies by wallet       |
| **Cryptocurrency** | 2%                | 10-60 minutes          |

<Tip>
  For international bank deposits or cash pick up, Whop charges an additional 1%.
</Tip>

## Dispute & risk management

Fees apply only when disputes occur or when you opt into premium protections (Early Dispute Alerts).

| Service                 | Fee  | When Applied                                                        |
| :---------------------- | :--- | :------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Dispute Fee**         | \$15 | Charged when a chargeback is filed against your account             |
| **Early Dispute Alert** | \$29 | Optional service to resolve disputes before they become chargebacks |

## Merchant of Record

When you sell through Whop, we act as the Merchant of Record. That means we handle all payment processing, chargebacks, sales tax, and compliance on your behalf.

<Note>
  - All fees are subject to change with 30 days notice
  - Foreign exchange (FX) rates are applied at current market rates for international transactions
  - Tax handling may vary by jurisdiction and transaction type
  - Some fees may be capped at certain amounts - see individual service terms for details
</Note>
