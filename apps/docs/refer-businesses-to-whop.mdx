title: "Refer businesses to Whop"
description: "Refer businesses to Whop and earn a commission."
---

If you refer a business to Whop, you'll earn a 30% commission on all future revenue they drive to our platform. Revenue is calculated on every transaction.

To refer businesses, go to the [Whop partners page](https://whop.com/affiliates/partners). You will be able to see a list of everyone you have referred.
