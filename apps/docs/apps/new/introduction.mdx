# What are Whop apps?

Whop apps allow you to build and embed your own software into whop.
Build your idea ontop of Whop's payments, authentication, and distribution infrastructure and
earn money as a developer.

It is your portal to programmatically access whop's data and functionality and provide custom user experiences within the whop ecosystem.
